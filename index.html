WHO
IS ATATÜRK

Mustafa Kemal Atatürk was born in 1881 (eighteen eighty one) in Salonica. His father’s name was Ali Rıza Bey and his mother was Zübeyde Hanım. Atatürk’s four out of five siblings died while they were young. His only sibling was his sister Makbule Hanım.
